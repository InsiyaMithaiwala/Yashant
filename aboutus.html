About Yashant

01

Vision

Yashant envisions fostering the development of a happy, healthy, and sustainable society in which every individual has an equal opportunity for growth and a life of dignity

02

Mission

Yashant is a youth-led futuristic organization committed to empowering the economically underprivileged by empowering them through encouragement, education, and employment. Yashant strives to encourage individuals to reach their full potential, lead happy, healthy lives with dignity and make them capable of making meaningful contributions to society. Yashant is shaping up a sustainable society for future generations by instilling futuristic ideals into our practices.

03

Values

⪢Transparency: Yashant ensures complete transparency in all our efforts and workings with Internal as well as external stakeholders of organisation.
⪢Respect: Yashant ensures respect towards each individual and their views and feelings inside out the organisation despite its cast, creed, gender or color.
⪢Integrity: Yashant works with integrity and ensures the dignity of all those involved in our endeavors. To comply with all laws, rules and regulations bestowed upon as part of an organisation.
⪢Empathy: To be empathetic to all those associated with Yashant and ensure a high sense of understanding of the feelings of all our stakeholders.
